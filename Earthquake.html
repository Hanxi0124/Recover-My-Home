Indonesian Lombok Earthquake

08/28/2018

Thousands of residents were left homeless after the massive earthquake struck an Indonesian island.

The Lombok Earthquake in Indonesia was an earthquake of magnitude 6.2, killing over 560 people and destroying 80% of buildings. It also displaced an estimated 20,000 people who fled to the mountains for fear of a tsunami. Landslides and debris made it difficult to provide food and water to the victims and for first aid trucks to get through.
