Guatemala Volcano Eruption

06/03/2018

Affected the mostly rural areas of Guatemala burning unfourtanate villages.

The Fire Volcano of Guatemala, had an eruption June 3 2018 and ws accompanied by avalanches that began at 9:20 p.m local time. It has a death toll of at least 107 and affecting 1.7 million people. Rescuers face hazardous conditions as they navigate hot, rocky debris. It expelled rock and gas, sending ash over 16,000 feet into the sky and interrupting search operations.
