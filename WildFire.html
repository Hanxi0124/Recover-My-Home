Wildfire in United States

06/03/2018

The the sixth-most destructive wildfire and the most destructive ever for Shasta County ignited July 23 2018.

The wildfire in California killed seven people, scorched more than 207,000 acres, an area larger than New York City. Flames have destroyed more than 1,000 homes in and around Redding, a town 200 miles north of San Francisco and home to 90,000 residents.
